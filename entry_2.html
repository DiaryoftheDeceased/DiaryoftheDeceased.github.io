Just as peace arrived, so did spring

And with her a new year

I could feel her in the ending months as I can now.

Beautiful she is, yet I want winter back.

The safe feeling of the dead trees and cold.

I age another year. One more until I am out of my youth.

Good. I would like to leave my youth as dead as I.

I shall dance in the castle along with the spirits.

A start I am eager to see.

Hmm, what will I do next?
