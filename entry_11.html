Entry 11

4•20•2024•12:30am

The castle is where I stay.

Out in the halls

Panic’s servant walks.

Every while; Once

She hums.

Front of my door.

She stops.

Taunting almost.

She knows the door has no lock.

I am safe.

Lest the door belongs open.

Thoughts of my own

My knight in armour.

I wonder

Do her ears know my heart’s song.
