Entry 23

5•4•2024 •3:50am•

By chance,

I was correct.

Time and time again,

In sleep,

I keep my soul alive.

My patience wanes.

Menevolent spirits.

Wishing ill on the famille.

I threatened magic.

At times,

They ceased to exist.

I was prepared.

The Grey Lady dressed in black.

It is comical.

 

I have been avoiding the dream world.

Yet I have ran out of reasons,

To journied past the Heliotropes.

I know little to none,

About what is past the Heliotropes.

I am void of expectations.

 

Going forth,

I shall journie past the Heliotropes,

To the cosmos.
