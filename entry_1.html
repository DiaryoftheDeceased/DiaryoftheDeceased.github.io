I'm awake in my castle.

It's dark, cold and brooding.

Sunlight peaks from the black curtains

Inviting itself without permission.

Each wall is the same.

Stone grey; Blank.

The room is minimal.

Almost empty.

I like it this way.

Less means worry is kept at bay.

I am happy at this moment.

I hope it lasts.
