Entry 25

5•7•2024•11:30pm

I wake.

Where I am.

I feel water.

No body of water near.

I was in bed.

 

On the floor.

Covered in blood.

No wound in sight.

 

Cracking of my skin.

I am weak.

Mara is gone.

Is that good or bad?

I am alone.

My soul is leaking out of my body.

Agony.

Soul and body shall stay one until death.

 

But

I am a ghost.

Am I not already dead?

 

Can it be, I was away from my domain for too long?

If i am not already dead,

I am dying.

 

Should I be like my essence and leave too?
