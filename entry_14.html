I am not locked up.

Yet feels like I am.

Eventually I will need to rid my unwanted visitor.

Till' then; I sit in my castle.

I have faced Panic before.

Though, at the time, I had help.

I just so happen to be where the Heliotropes are.

Succumbing to Panic

Asking for help

All makes me feel like a hopeless child.

Thought is right.

I will become weak if I let Panic take over what is mine.
