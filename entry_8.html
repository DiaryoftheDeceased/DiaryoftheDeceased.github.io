I am writing this out.

‘Fore my mind and forget become acquaintances.

I visit the diary of the deceased often.

So it shan't be.

I want to explore the forest west of the castle.

It is quite bothersome.

Yet to have found reason.

I have journied once far west of the castle.

Only curious of course.

Til’ I laid eyes on the forest.

I wonder what it hides?

Nothing?

Charm?

I will not know unless I gather my legs and feet

To leave the castle.
