4•8•2024•2:25am

Yesterday was a celestial event.

I got to see the sun and the moon cross paths.

It was magical and unsettling.

What’s to come after the celestial event

Only an oracle can tell.

I may visit my friendly stranger in the Heliotrope plain.

They keep appearing in sleep.

Secretly I wish it to cease.

I do not wish to look into their life without permission.

Conversing is a penny to none.

I will speak and then leave for another while.

It displeases me that my friendly stranger is always in the Heliotrope plain.

Than in the waking.

I am unknowledgeable on how to stop it.

My forte is spirits.

In the meantime, I may just have to suffer.
