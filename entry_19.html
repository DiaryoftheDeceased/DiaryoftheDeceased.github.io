Entry 19

4•26•2024•2:04am

In my life,

Nothing is new.

I do not need an oracle to tell me

I would see her again.

Yet I wish she had died,

When I unsuccessfully killed her.

 

She stares and paces.

We were close as children.

I changed and wanted her dead.

 

“You are not going to speak a word to me?”

She tells.

 

I have nothing to say to her.

We are no longer alike.

Like a knife to flesh.

Such is life and death,

When you have a body.
