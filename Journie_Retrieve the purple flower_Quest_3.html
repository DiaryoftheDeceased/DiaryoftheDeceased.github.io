Quest 3

05•14•2024•11:42pm

The cosmos will not unhand me.

I am slow to anger,

Yet this dilemma has used its feet to run on a course.

I have been trapped for days.

I have not seen my castle, Mara, or the sky.

This is getting concerning

 

Dreams repeatedly showing,

Watching from afar.

My friendly stranger,

Not being seen.

Over and over again.

 

I know the lesson.

I am drained.

I can only imagine,

how much essense I have lost in the waking.

I have wandered the modern realm.

For me,

This is dangerous.

 

To be gone for so long.

I do not crack in other realms but my own.

Am I a ghost?

 

I need to think.

I have not yet to leave.

I turn to the cosmos.

 

No.

 

If the cosmos will not let me leave,

It will not help me leave.

I walk the area.

I come across a field.

Littered with this realms signature.

You can see the moon & the sun.

Purple.

Blue.

Pink.

Orange.

All share the same sky.

The stars.

 

The stars are the way home.

The stars have been used for centuries.

To trace the sky's memories,

Is to trace one’s path home.

 

I need to find my stars.

I lay in the field.

I close my eyes.

I meditate.

I speak the spell.

 

I become my ethel.

My noble form.

My highest form.

 

Here,

I am honest.

Here,

I know I will get the definite truth.

 

I am bask in gold and pearls.

I wear a small gown that is of linen.

White.

A hue I admire.

Here,

I do not need aid for my sight.

I have three eyes,

Six wings,

the darkest hue that sparkles white.

 

It feels divine to be.

 

Matte Gold waters and opalescent clouds.

 

One would think heaven.

 

I think true.

 

Leap to and from stone in the water.

I look up,

 

I think.

“Why do you show me the same storie repeatedly?”

 

A loud ringing.

My ears weep.

It shakes the area.

It shakes the spirit.

 

“FIND WHO YOU ARE.

LETTING GO.

YOU DO NOT WANT IT TO BE TRUE.

THIS HAS TO BE.

THE NEXT CHAPTER IS READY SET FORTH.

IT IS YOU WHO ALL WAIT.”

 

The ringing ceases.

I am whisked back to the cosmos.

 

I have received what I have already been told.

 

I start to run.

I become like a horse.

The winds aid my legs

I dash to the nearest ledge,

I jump.

 

It is about a building high.

In mid air,

With my back to the earth.

I close my eyes.

I should be awake now.
