In the castle is fine.

Yesterday was once in forever.

The dark fog arrived.

Then the silhouettes wandered into the castle.

The silhouettes whispered:

“Come play with us. You know who we are. Grab the daggers and swords. Together, let us go to another realm.”

It's a miracle I had not listen.

Had I, I would have been back at the playground again.

It's a place I have worked so hard at escaping.

Sometimes I worry if I will ever close the gate to the playground.
