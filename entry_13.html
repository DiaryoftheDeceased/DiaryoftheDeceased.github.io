Entry 13

4•21•2024•2:17am

I can not leave the castle yet.

Panic’s servant still walks.

So I shall stay put.

My thoughts and I converse.

We talk about all sorts.

This realm.

Famille.

My friendly Stranger.

I encourage thought not to overthink.

I speak rather calmly to her.

She asks specifically.

She tells:

“What is to become of you, if a mere servant is preventing you from leaving the very place you have created?”

( … )

I can only offer her a blank stare and silence.

Thought had created an epiphany.

And gifted it to me.

For she is clever.
