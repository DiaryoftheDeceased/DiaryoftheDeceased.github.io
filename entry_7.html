Entry 7

4•13•2024•6:30am

Today was fine, although sleep did not want me.

A renaissance swept my thoughts.

The atmosphere declines.

A knock at the castle door.

I open to a child.

Sweet and smiling.

I do not feel safe.

She hisses a word:

 

“Regress.”

Flickering; contorting herself seven feet above.

Vengefully dark.

I do not get visitors.

I need a sharp object.

It needs to unhand me.

It is screaming louder.

Clawing.

Begging.

Weeping.

At me.

Scissors.

I cut into her.

Wounds.

My bloody hands.

Her.

And my scissors.

Falling dead ever so gently.

I stare as life fades; Unfeeling.

It has-had to be done.

It can not be here.

There is no place for her now.

I do not regret what I am going to do-have done.
