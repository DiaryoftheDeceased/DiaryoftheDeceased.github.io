Entry 12

4•20•2024•6:43pm

I yearn to give words to someone.

That of my world

Though I worry

For they may not understand.

Or believe.

I say it is not a problem.

Yet being formed

Around formless soulettes.

One has formed in this realm.

How far they have traveled.

Only to have a name

A gift

Beauty

As well as darkness they keep leashed.
