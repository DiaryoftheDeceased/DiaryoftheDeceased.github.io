This day was oddly joyous.

I was challenged

As well, gained a mortal foe.

The Mighty Duck.

Do not ever offer your back to foe.

It attacked.

I fled.

Only for me to return with risk and defense.

I embodied bravery to guide me.

 

There were children near.

In need of their plaything.

They were then first priority.

Me and defense stood against the Mighty Duck.

With Risk and bravery at our sides.

The Mighty Duck had backed down.

We welcomed vitory

And took home few battle scars.

 

I understood the attack.

The Mighty Duck was to be a father.

In the face of danger I laughed and was unbothered.

It displays how I have surpassed and grown.
