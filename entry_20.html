Entry 20

4•28•2024 •4:20am•

Two days beyond us.

I now have a twin.

Followed to and fro the land.

She has tried to speak with me.

But I still do not engage.

 

Her eyes are daggers.

She is a muse to anyone’s eyes.

For the fool may not know better.

Beware the veil she possesses.

I encourage the delay of the inevitable.
